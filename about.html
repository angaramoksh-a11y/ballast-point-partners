About Ballast Point Partners

Steady hands for the whole voyage.

Since 2001, Ballast Point Partners has provided expansion capital to established but rapidly growing private companies across the Southeast US and Texas — taking minority positions and partnering with founders, never taking control.

Who We Are

100% dedicated to helping founders realize their dreams.

Ballast Point Partners is a Tampa, Florida growth equity firm. Our principals have spent their careers building and investing in outstanding companies, and we view that long industry experience as the means for helping a company with a proven business model accelerate its growth.

We provide expansion capital of $10M–$50M to rapidly growing companies in software, tech-enabled services, and healthcare. We take minority positions — we don’t buy control of your business or tell you how to run it. When you partner with us, our expertise goes to work, investing in you and supporting you with a trusted, knowledgeable, skilled team.

“We know there will be bumps along the way. If you call us with a problem, we’re going to help you get through it. Together, we’ve got this.”
