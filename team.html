Partners

The partnership.

Five partners with careers built at South Atlantic Venture Funds, Morgan Stanley, Raymond James, Goldman Sachs and Wachovia — investing together across the Southeast and Texas.

Drew Graham
Managing Partner & Co-Founder

Co-founded Ballast Point Ventures in 2002 after six years at South Atlantic Venture Funds, where he was a Principal and General Partner in Funds III and IV. Began his private-equity career in Morgan Stanley's Merchant Banking Division and as a consultant at the Parthenon Group. Currently Chairman of Theragen; previously Chairman of KBI BioPharma, kSep Holdings, MolecularMD and SleepMed. Also Chairman of the Board of Tampa General Hospital.

Princeton University (Phi Beta Kappa, magna cum laude) · Harvard MBA, with Distinction

Paul Johan
Partner & Chief Operating Officer

Co-founded Ballast Point Ventures in 2002 after four years in the Investment Banking Group of Raymond James & Associates, where he led the Telecommunications Equipment practice and helped clients raise over $2 billion. Serves on the boards of Numeracle, AVOXI, The Zebra, Dash Solutions and Vazata, and on the University of Tampa Board of Trustees.

Emory University · Darden Graduate School of Business (University of Virginia) MBA

Matt Rice
Partner

Joined Ballast Point Partners in 2004 and leads much of the firm's healthcare investing. Serves on the boards of Medsender, careviso, PaceMate, Suncoast Skin Solutions, IRIS, Theragen and BioTissue, and as a Board Observer at Carterra. Began his career in the Healthcare Investment Banking Group of Raymond James and worked in Strategic Alliances at the Novartis Institutes for BioMedical Research.

University of Virginia (Commerce) · Harvard Business School MBA

Robert Faber
Partner

Joined Ballast Point Partners in 2010. Serves on the boards of PowerChord, Symphonic Distribution, Mailprotector, Key Data, Gun.io, Sojo and Work Shield, and as a Board Observer at Spotio. Previously an Associate at Wachovia Capital Partners (now Pamlico Capital) in Charlotte, focused on media, communications, business services and energy.

Princeton University · Tuck School of Business at Dartmouth MBA

Sean Barkman
Partner

Joined Ballast Point Partners in 2011. Serves on the boards of Abacode, Spotio, Keen Decision Systems and Viirtue. Previously spent three years in the Technology Investment Banking Group at Raymond James, active in both M&A and capital raising, and has served on the Board of the Florida Venture Forum.

University of Central Florida (Finance, summa cum laude) · Crummer Graduate School of Business at Rollins College MBA

Jerett Smith
Vice President

Joined Ballast Point Partners in 2018 from the Healthcare Investment Banking Group at Goldman Sachs, where he was active in mergers and acquisitions, equity capital markets and shareholder advisory.

University at Buffalo (Delta Sigma Pi Scholarship Key) · Stanford Graduate School of Business MBA

Laura Stein
Vice President

Washington University in St. Louis (Environmental Studies) · Kellogg School of Management (Northwestern) MBA
